The existence of a maṅgalaśloka here in what appears to be the middle of the text is not going unnoted by the two commentators (śiṣṭācāraprāptaṃ maṅgalaṃ granthamadhye racayati […]). Since the oldest available manuscript from Vārāṇāsī has a only three chapters, this might indicate that the fourth chapter was an addition.
